# IIS access 로그 조회(LogParser)

- 경로 : C:\Program Files (x86)\Log Parser 2.2

## 사용예제

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT date time c-ip cs-uri-stem FROM ‘iis access log path’ WHRE time-taken \> 500” -o:CSV

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT date time c-ip cs-uri-stem FROM iis_access_log_path to C:\temp\output.csv WHRE time-taken \> 500” -o:CSV

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT date time c-ip cs-uri-stem FROM iis_access_log_path WHRE sc-status=200” -o:CSV

### 특정 시간 사이

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT TO_LOCALTIME(TO_TIMESTAMP(EXTRACT_PREFIX(TO_STRING(\[#Fields: date-time\]), 0,'T'), ‘yyyy-MM-dd’)) AS LogDate FROM ‘iis_access_log_path’ WHRE time-taken \> 500” -o:CSV

### 장애 발생 추정 시간대

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT date, time, cs-uri-stem FROM d:\temp\accesslog\\.log to d:\temp\accesslog\result_request.csv WHERE TO_TIMESTAMP(date, time) BETWEEN TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 04:30:00', ‘yy/MM/dd hh:mm:ss’) AND TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 05:35:00', ‘yy/MM/dd hh:mm:ss’)”

### 장애 발생 추정 시간대 request group 별 발생 횟수

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT cs-uri-stem AS UrlWithoutParams, COUNT(\*) AS Total FROM d:\temp\accesslog\\.log to d:\temp\accesslog\result_request.csv WHERE TO_TIMESTAMP(date, time) BETWEEN TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 04:30:00', ‘yy/MM/dd hh:mm:ss’) AND TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 05:35:00', ‘yy/MM/dd hh:mm:ss’) GROUP BY cs-uri-stem ORDER BY Total DESC”

### 정상 시간대 request

- LogParser -i:IISW3C “SELECT TO_LOCALTIME(TO_TIMESTAMP(date, time)) as local-timestamp, c-ip, cs-method, cs-uri-stem, time-taken FROM d:\temp\accesslog\\.log to d:\temp\accesslog\result_request_normal.csv WHERE TO_TIMESTAMP(date, time) BETWEEN TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 06:00:00', ‘yy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss’) AND TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 07:00:00', ‘yy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss’)”

### 정상 시간대 request group별 발생 횟수

- LogParser.exe -i:IISW3C “SELECT cs-uri-stem AS UrlWithoutParams, COUNT(\*) AS Total FROM d:\temp\accesslog\\.log to d:\temp\accesslog\result_request_normal.csv WHERE TO_TIMESTAMP(date, time) BETWEEN TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 06:00:00', ‘yy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss’) AND TIMESTAMP('24/01/04 07:00:00', ‘yy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss’) GROUP BY cs-uri-stem ORDER BY Total DESC”
